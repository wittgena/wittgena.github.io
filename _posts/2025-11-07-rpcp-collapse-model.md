In tightly coupled socio-economic systems, it is no longer necessary for a massive war, blackout, or earthquake to trigger collapse.

- fractures cultural narratives (Mythos),
- triggers reflexive financial reactions (Phronesis),
- saturates derivative structures (ETFs, leverage, protocol rules),
- and overwhelms the executional reality itself.

This is not hypothetical.  
It is the **actual dynamic unfolding** across financial markets, media systems, and political orders.

> The loop feeds itself.
